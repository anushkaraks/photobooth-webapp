## Space-Themed Photobooth Web App

Step into a tiny corner of the universe where your camera becomes a spaceship!!

This space-themed photobooth web app lets you snap photos, upload photos, and enjoy a smooth, playful experience wrapped in a cosmic aesthetic. From landing on the homepage to capturing your moment and viewing the final result, every step is designed to feel like a mini journey through space.

It’s not just about taking pictures, it’s about turning a simple click into a fun, interactive experience.
